Privacy Policy

Effective July 14, 2026

Bridge Trainer stores practice progress and preferences on your device. We do not require an account and do not sell personal information.

Purchases are processed by Apple. RevenueCat receives purchase and device-related identifiers needed to provide Bridge+ access and restore purchases. Its handling of that data is governed by its privacy policy.

Reminder notifications are scheduled locally on your device. You can disable them in the app or in iOS Settings.
