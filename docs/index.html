Bridge practice for newer players

Bridge Trainer

Build better instincts in five minutes at a time, from card basics to bidding, declarer play, and defense.

Learn the table without needing a table.

Short, focused drills make contract bridge easier to return to every day. Work through original examples, see the reasoning, and build confidence one decision at a time. Bridge Trainer uses a beginner Standard American framework. Partnership agreements vary, so treat conventions as a starting point, not a universal rulebook.

Card basics

Get comfortable with suits, ranks, tricks, and the vocabulary that makes every later lesson easier.

Opening bids

Practice first decisions with clear explanations for beginner Standard American bidding.

Declarer play

Choose a plan, count your winners, and learn to turn a contract into a sequence of manageable plays.

Defense

Build good habits for opening leads, signals, and the keep-or-switch decisions that shape a hand.
