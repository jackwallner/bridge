Bridge Trainer Support

Bridge Trainer teaches card basics, beginner Standard American opening bids, declarer play, and defense with short drills.

Frequently asked questions

Why can bidding advice differ from my club?
Bridge partnerships use different agreements. The app presents a beginner Standard American framework and explains the assumption behind each answer.

How do I restore Bridge+?
Open Settings, choose Bridge+, then tap Restore Purchases. Use the same Apple Account used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel a subscription?
Manage subscriptions in your Apple Account settings. Apple handles billing and cancellation.
