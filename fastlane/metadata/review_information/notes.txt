Bridge Trainer is a contract bridge practice app. It contains flashcards, quizzes, opening-bid hand evaluation, and card-play scenarios. There is no live multiplayer, wagering, or user-generated content.

HOW TO FIND THE FOUR FREE PRACTICE ROOMS
1. On first launch, tap Get Started on the final onboarding page.
2. On the feature tour, tap Skip the tour, take me to the app. If the tour is completed, dismiss the sample session first.
3. On Home, scroll to the THE ROOMS section. The first four cards are free and tappable: The Card Room, The Auction Room, The Declarer Room, and The Defense Room.
4. Tap any room card, then tap its unlocked drill, such as Meet the Game, Opening Playbook, Declarer Playbook, or Lead or Hold?. No purchase is required.

All four beginner rooms are free. Bridge+ adds one extra practice set in each room and unlocks the Master Tables. The RevenueCat entitlement is “Bridge+”.

The app uses a beginner Standard American framework. Bridge conventions are partnership agreements, and the app states this in Settings and on Home.

To review purchases, open Settings, choose Get Bridge+, and use Apple sandbox purchasing. Restore Purchases is available on the same screen.
